School Shooting Database (1970-present)

Data Source

K-12 School Shooting Database

Number of school shooting incidents: 1300+

Time Period: 1970 ~ present

Summary

  • School shooting incidents cluster in New England, South, Midwest, and SoCal. States with most shooting incidents: CA (158), TX (133), FL (91), MI (67), and IL (64).
  • Most of the shooting incidents took place in Morning Classes and then Evening.
  • The number of school shooting incidents gradually increased from 1970 to the present. Peaks: 1988 (38), 1993 (42), 2006 (59), 2018 (97)
  • There is a direct correlation between a shooting incident and a spike in search for related terms.
  • The search of Gun related queries increases a month after the actual incident
